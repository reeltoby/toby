Project Abstract

Current popular weather services in Ontario provide the user with weather information corresponding to the closest available weather station. As is often the case, the closest weather station to a given location can be many kilometers away. When this is the case, the user’s weather information has the potential to be vastly different than reality. To fix this problem, Owen Andrews-Simons of Arubrum Innovations has asked our group to develop a product that can deliver an estimation of the true weather at the user’s exact location (or chosen location). Additionally, we have been tasked to use only open-source technologies and solutions (no- paid licenses) that can provide a new report every hour.
